Q&A

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How much time in advance do you need to place an order?

    We can generally accommodate small orders, like daily store items with 24 hours notice. larger store cakes should be ordered at least 1 week in advance.

    We have a very small staff and space so We highly suggest placing your order as soon as you know the details of your event to avoid disappointment.

  • How should I start the order?

    The best way to begin the order is to email us at megansbakery@gmail.com and include details.

    You can always stop into our store to discuss your order as well.

  • If I have a photo of a cake that I love, can you replicate it?

    We love when our customers come prepared with inspiration for their order.

    Generally we are also working with slightly different sizes and materials to fit the specifics of your order so we sometimes need to alter the design to better suit your event!

  • If I want a specific image on my cake, can you print an edible image for it?

    We can accommodate edible images on cakes however we require at least 2 weeks notice for the order

  • Do you deliver?

    We can deliver however we need at least a week's notice to schedule deliveries. All of our deliveries have a specific fee dependent on the distance we need to travel to your venue.

  • If I'm interested in tasting your cakes before ordering. What should I do?

    We would love to have you into the bakery for a tasting and consultation with our owner. Please email us to start the process!!
